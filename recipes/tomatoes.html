Homegrown Sundried Tomatoes

Description

Are you, like, really wondering how to make sundried tomatoes? It's right there in the name. You just have to make sure they were grown near your Custom Woodfire Stone Oven. Trust me, you can tell if they weren't raised near one long enough.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes from your garden
  • Woodfire Sone Oven
  • Greek Stone Slab
  • The Sun

Steps

  1. Grow your tomatoes if you haven't already.
  2. Make sure your Custom Woodfire Stone Oven is within 50ft of your growing tomatoes.
  3. While they are growing, you will want to run your Custom Woodfire Stone Oven at least once every few days. This will help it and the tomatoes to better attune to each other. This step is very important. The better attuned they are, the better your Custom Woodfire Stone Oven will be able to cook them.
  4. Once the tomatoes have ripened, pick them and place them on your Greek Stone Slab and let them sit in the sun for as long as Achilles fought in the Trojan War after his closest companion Patroclus perished pretending to be him.
  5. Enjoy.
