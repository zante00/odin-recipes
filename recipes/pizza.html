Ingredients

  • Do it yourself homegrown sundried tomatoes
  • Locally grown mushrooms picked from your local forest
  • 5 different craft cheeses bought from an authentic Frenchmen
  • I can't believe it's not butter

  • Custom Woodfire Stone Oven crafted by an Italian masonry expert

Steps

  1. Pre heat your Custom Woodfire Stone Oven to around 450 or so degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Flaten your dough into a circle.
  3. Make a ring around the edge with the butter and cheddar.
  4. Roll the edge of the dough over the ring.
  5. Coat the rest of the dough with the Homemade Alfredo Sauce.
  6. Sprinkle your Homegrown Sundried Tomatoes and Mushrooms on top.
  7. Coat as liberally as your grandchild with all 5 cheeses.
  8. Sprinkle lightly with garlic.
  9. Place in Custom Woodfire Stone Oven for 20 minutes.
  10. Pull out on time like your father should have done and enjoy.
