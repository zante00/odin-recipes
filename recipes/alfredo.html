Homemade Alfredo Sauce

Description

So you, like, don't even know how to make alfredo sauce. Understandable, it is difficult. I CAN make it on my own, but Susan down the street makes it sooooo much better so, like, just buy her's. Seriously, no matter how hard you try you won't be able to come close to her's. So save yourself the time and buy Susan's alfredo sauce. It's only $5 too. It's basically a steal. But don't, like, actually steal it because, chances are, you most likely got your Chicago dough from her sister.

Ingredients

  • Susan
  • A car, if you live a bit away
  • $5, seriously, she's crazy, she could make a fortune off of her alfredo sauce

Steps

  1. From the gas station at the corner of 53rd and Main.
  2. Go right up north on Main until you get to Charleston.
  3. 3 houses past Charleston is a driveway with a gate, pull up and stay in your car.
  4. Keep you hands on the wheel at all times until told otherwise.
  5. When the security guard greets you, tell him "Mary is hungry".
  6. He will ask for the money, slowly present him with the $5.
  7. He will leave with it and return with a jar of Susan's alfredo sauce.
  8. Go home and enjoy.
